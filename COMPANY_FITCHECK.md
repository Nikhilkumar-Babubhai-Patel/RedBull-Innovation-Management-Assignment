# COMPANY FITCHECK

## Company Familiarity
Red Bull is a globally recognized brand known for its energy drink products. Since its inception in 1984, Red Bull has established itself as a leader in the energy drink market, promoting a lifestyle associated with extreme sports and high energy.

## Product Selection
The product selected for this assignment is the Red Bull Energy Drink. It is a market leader in the energy drink category and is known for its unique taste and branding that appeals to a younger demographic.

## Sector Classification
Red Bull operates in the Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) sector. FMCG products are characterized by high turnover rates and relatively low margins, and Red Bull fits this classification as it is widely available in various retail outlets and consumed regularly.

## Source Availability
Red Bull's sourcing includes various ingredients characterized by high consumer demand, ensuring a steady supply chain. The major components like caffeine, taurine, and B-vitamins are both sourced globally and locally, which enhances their accessibility and reduces supply chain risks.
