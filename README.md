# Red Bull Innovation Management Assignment

## Assignment Title
Innovation Management Strategies for Red Bull

## Company Overview
Red Bull GmbH is an Austrian company that is well-known for its energy drinks and expands into various sectors including sports, music, and various forms of entertainment. Founded in 1984, it has established a significant global market presence and is recognized for its innovative marketing campaigns and support of extreme sports.
